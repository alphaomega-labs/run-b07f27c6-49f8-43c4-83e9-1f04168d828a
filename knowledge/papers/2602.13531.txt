B.3       Kernel hyper-parameters tuning
For each task, we select the Matérn kernel hyper-parameters once
using a lightweight train/validation procedure, and then keep them
fixed across all reported runs for that task. The tuner is intentionally
simple (single split and basic optimizers) to keep the overhead
minimal compared to the cost of quantum feature.
